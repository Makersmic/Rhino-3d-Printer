The two components that make up the center idler assembly.  Two of each part should be printed and then bolted together with 2 m3x75 bolts and 2 m3 hex-head bolts.  
Optimized for a 15mm pulley that has a total height of 19mm, one idler assembly bolts to the top of the frame while the other bolts to the bottom, using the previously mentioned 
fasteners.  Refer to included drawing for additional details.
